Chicken Ranch Wraps

Requirements-you will need 8 thin slices cooked Chicken, 4 flour tortillas (6 inches) room temperature, 1 large tomato thinly sliced, 1 medium green pepper, cut into thin strips, 1 cup shredded lettuce, 1 cup shredded cheddar cheese, 1/3 cup ranch salad dressing .

Recipe

Place 2 slices of turkey on each tortilla. Layer with tomato, green pepper, lettuce and cheese. Drizzle with salad dressing. Roll up tightly. Nutrition Facts- 403 calories, 25g fat, 19g carbohydrate, 26g protein.
Disclaimer: Never leave a child unattended in the kitchen. Limit the child to tasks that are safe and age-appropriate.
